List of Kenya Vehicle Manufacturers with Contacts

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

Most Kenyans want to own a car or upgrade from the one they already has. This aspiration is one that warrants a third eye to escape cons in the industry, save money, and get the best quality for every vehicle you vouch for. Dealing with reliable vehicle manufacturers opens a new world of opportunities. For instance, you might walk in as a customer and come out with huge business ideas. Well, here are the top Kenya vehicle manufacturers that you may want to deal with.

List of vehicle manufacturers in Kenya

Here is a list of the top vehicle manufacturers in Kenya:

Kenya Vehicle Manufacturers

This company began production in 1976. Since then, the Kenya vehicle manufacturers limited has been a major influencer in the vehicle manufacturing in Kenya. To date, they have cumulatively rolled out 60,000 vehicles. Originally, KVM was established as a manufacturer of both light and heavy commercial vehicles, but today they have inclined more on bus body building and vehicle assembly.

Milestone Cars ASV

Established in 2000, this company is known internationally for its exquisite development and unmatched supply of armored vehicles. With major offices in different continents; Africa, Europe and Asia, Milestone Cars is a name you can’t skip when mentioning the giants in Kenya vehicle manufacturers association.

Apart from the common armored cars for the security and defense industry, Milestone Cars also make custom vehicles for specific needs, for example, cash-in-transit, armored ambulances, armored personnel carrier and armored emergency vehicles. We know how hard it is to get Kenya vehicle manufacturers’ contacts so we availed this too. It is located in Moi Avenue, Mombasa Call, +254 (0) 722 252 477, +971 (0) 529 114 078.

MSPV Armoured vehicles

Minerva Special Purpose Vehicles (MSPV) specializes in custom-made vehicles for both military and civilian use. For military use, they manufacture armored SUVs and light military trucks.

To contact them, visit their website: www.mspv.com. You can also call; +254 202 400318, +97148830444.

Crater Automobiles

If you have never spotted this on a truck “Another One from Crater Automobiles”, then you haven’t seen anything. Yeah, Crater Automobiles is a vehicle manufacturing company that is known for its powerful trucks. This company is based in Kisumu and does vehicle sales and service too.

Contact them through this number; (254) 57 427 75

LABHSONS

This company based in Nairobi not only deals in vehicle manufacturing and sales but also deals in transport and other motor services. Though there is little information about the number of people they employ, you could check it for Kenya vehicle manufacturers jobs; you never know when an opportunity might surface. Contact: 254) 20 54 06 36.

BANBROS

Well, well if you are in need of bus manufacturers with a proven track record of reliability and quality, you have definitely hit the right button. Banbros is one of the leading manufacturers of buses that are “Engineered for passion”.

Their coaches are made for luxury. Visit them for institutional buses, Lorries, and vans. They also make custom design vehicles. Contact: (254) 20 54 10 11

Agro Manufacturing

Agro manufacturing emerged in the Kenya vehicle manufacturers’ scene in the late ‘80s. Then, their key focus was on the manufacturing of light and heavy trucks for commercial purposes. Since its inception, Agro Manufacturing has rolled out 60,000 vehicles cumulatively. It has since spread its services to neighboring countries like Rwanda, Uganda, Congo, Malawi, and Sudan among other African countries.

They are based in Industrial Area, Nairobi. Call; 0712 84 89 54 / 0791 578 297 Email: info@agrotruckbodies.com
