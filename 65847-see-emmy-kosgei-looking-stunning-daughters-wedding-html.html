Emmy Kosgei And Husband At Sandra Madubuko's Wedding

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Celebrated gospel star Emmy Kosgei has shared images of her family at their daughter's wedding in Nigeria, which went down on Saturday November 14, 2015.

The 35-year-old Taunet Nelel singer has been married to Nigeria’s Revival Assembly preacher Apostle Anslem Madubuko for two years now, after the two met at a church ministry event.

Madubuko has two children, Sandra and Velma, from a previous marriage, who Emmy embraced as her own, when the two tied the knot.

Last Saturday was Sandra’s wedding, where Emmy was looking stunning donning a Nigerian gele and a designer outfit.

The award-winning singer's family back in Kenya also travelled to attend the function, as Emmy herself relocated to Nigeria to live with her husband.

The first Madubuko daughter Velma was married in 2012, the year her mother and first wife of the pastor Connie Madubuko passed on.

She welcomed a baby in May 2015, making Apostle Madubuko a grandfather and Emmy a grandmother to the pretty baby girl.
