See How Tuface Celebrated Daughter At Seven (PHOTOS)

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, May 3, 2024

Nigerian music icon Tuface Idibia could not hide his joy as his daughter Isabella turned seven years old on Thursday, December 10.

In celebrating her first born, Tuface's wife, Annie Idibia, posted a collage of pictures of Isabella on Instagram with the caption: “My SunShine… My good luck charm … My Mine.. So loving, so caring, so thoughtful for her age.. Great big sista.. My beyond amazing Daughter is 7 today!! Happy birthday Sunshine !!! Mama is so proud !!! #iHaveThingsILongThroat4 #mysunshine #birthdaygirl.”

Being a week day, Tuface decided to pay a surprise visit to his daughter in school. Isabella, who was dressed in a pink sleeveless baby doll gown, sat in front of her class with a birthday cake as her father came in singing his hit song African Queen.

He remixed the song to suit the day, singing: “Isabella, you are my African queen, the girl of my dreams, the only one I’ve ever seen with a smile so bright."

Annie also posted a short video of the touching scene on her Instagram. She captioned it:“And her first love sang to her… #mySunshine #bdaygirl.”
