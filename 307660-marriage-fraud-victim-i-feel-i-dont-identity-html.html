Woman loses identity after mysterious man registers her as his legal wife

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, July 20, 2024

A South African woman was eagerly waiting to participate in the country's 2006 election just like any other patriotic citizen would.

However, Nomathamsanqa Sweetness Swartbooi, while registering to vote, was shocked to find out she was legally married to a man she had never met.

Speaking to Eyewitness News, the woman who now says she no longer has an identity, claimed the mysterious man went on to seal deals using her name.

According to her, the man used her surname to open accounts, take loans and even register two children to her name.

"I feel like I no longer have an identity," said the woman.

Immediately after the discovery, Swartbooi went to seek help and resolve the issue at the country's Ministry of Home Affairs.

She said that the system was checked and confirmed that she was indeed married to the mysterious man.

After several attempts to have her name cleared, the department eventually dismissed her.

To worsen her situation, the woman delivered her second baby in 2008 but she could not register him at Home Affairs.

She claimed the boy is now 11-years old and without a birth certificate a situation which worries her in the unfortunate incident that she died.

"The person somewhere there is enjoying all that should be rightfully mine," she said.

The devastated woman can neither register her marriage with her husband because of her compromised ID number.

She is also struggling to find work as a result of bad credit due to the loans taken by the person she is legally 'married to'.
