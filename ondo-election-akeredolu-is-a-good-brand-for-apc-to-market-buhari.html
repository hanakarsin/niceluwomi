President Buhari Reveals Why Akeredolu Should Be Re-Elected

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, March 8, 2024

President Muhammadu Buhari has said that he is delighted by Ondo State Governor, Rotimi Akerodolu for his ability to ‘perform well’ within the limits of available resources.

According to the president, Akeredolu is a good brand for the All Progressive Congress to market for the people of Ondo State. Buhari again urged Ondo residents to re-elect the governorship candidate for the good work he commenced in his first tenure.

The president boasted that he is confident that Akeredolu will again receive huge support from the people of Ondo in the coming election.

Buhari stated this in a post on his verified Twitter account on Wednesday, October 7, Naija News understands.

The tweet reads, “I have no doubt that Governor @RotimiAkeredolu is a good brand for the APC to market. I am particularly delighted by his ingenious approach to governance. His performance within the limits of available resources is endearing.”

“Governor Akeredolu deserves to be re-elected to consolidate the good work he commenced in his first term. I am not just proud of his achievements; I am confident he will enjoy the mandate of the good people of Ondo State for the second term.”
