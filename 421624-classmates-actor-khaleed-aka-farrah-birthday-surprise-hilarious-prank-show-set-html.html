Classmates Actor Khaleed Aka Farrah Gets Birthday Surprise After Hilarious Prank on Show Set

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Tuesday, July 9, 2024
  • Classmates actor Khaleed Abdul alias Farrah got a rude shock when a woman accused her of impregnating and ignoring her
  • The actor was on set filming the show when the woman barged in with her brother
  • After a tense encounter, it turned out to be a prank as they presented him with a birthday cake

Actor Khaleed Abdul alias Farrah has been pranked by his castmates on set during his birthday.

Farrah was filming an episode of Classmates on Friday, August 6 when a woman walked in with her brother, claiming he had impregnated her.

Accusations

The woman identified Farrah by pointing at him, leaving the actor stunned.

“This guy impregnated my sister and refused to take responsibility,” the visibly angry man said.

Taken aback, the actor inquired whether the woman accusing him of impregnating her was insane.

A debate ensued as Farrah sought to find out who the woman is.

"Is she crazy to come here and point you out? What sort of man are you?" The brother posed before shoving Farrah.

The woman claiming to be pregnant for the actor was then asked to leave the room as they continued arguing.

“Who are you? I don’t even know that woman,” Farrah defended himself.

The woman who had accused Farrah of making her pregnant then walked into the room carrying a cake, and they all started singing for him.

The actor shared a video of the prank on social media with the caption:

“My ninjas gave me an early birthday surprise. I don’t like surprises, love you guys so much. And happy birthday to me.”
