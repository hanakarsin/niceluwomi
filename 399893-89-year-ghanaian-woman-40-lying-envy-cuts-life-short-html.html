89-year-old woman advises young people to avoid lies and envy so as to live long

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, February 24, 2024

- Yesu Mogya, an 89-year-old woman who looks like she's in her 40s, revealed the secrets to her youthful appearance

- According to her, she totally avoids envy, theft, jealousy, anger and also never eats street food

- The aged woman whose name means 'Blood of Jesus' also narrated a real-life miracle from which her name emerged

An 89-year-old Ghanaian woman called Yesu Mogya has given priceless tips on longevity and how she has been able to keep looking forever-young.

The woman who looks as though she is in her forties said her grandparent lived up to 140 years and gave her tips on longevity which she has applied until now.

According to Yesu Mogya, she never holds grudges in her heart, never harbours jealousy or envy, does not lie, steal or backbite.

Apart from these, the 89-year-old woman mentioned that she also never eats street food as they are of low quality and are usually contaminated with poisonous chemicals from their packaging.
