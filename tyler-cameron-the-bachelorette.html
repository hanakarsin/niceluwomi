Who Is Tyler Cameron From 'The Bachelorette'? Get to Know the Hot Model

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, February 9, 2024

'Bachelorette' Fans Are Already Mildly Obsessed With Model Tyler Cameron

By Allison Cacich

Apr. 29 2019, Updated 5:01 p.m. ET

As fans of The Bachelorette prepare for Hannah Brown to take the reins in Season 15, we’re getting up close and personal with the 33 men who will be competing for the blonde beauty’s heart starting on May 13.

Based on the guys’ cast photographs, Tyler Cameron from Jupiter, Fla., has already become a favorite among viewers. Scroll down for more information about the uber attractive 26-year-old, including where to find him on Instagram!

Tyler played football in college.

According to Reality Steve, the tall, dark, and handsome athlete served as Wake Forest University’s backup QB for two years before transferring to Florida Atlantic University where he switched to wide receiver. 

On his Instagram, which is currently set to private, Tyler states that he graduated from WFU before receiving an MBA from FAU. 

In a 2014 article, Tyler’s football coach at Wake, Dave Clawson, revealed why he was let go from the team. "Tyler has had some injuries, and he has indicated to us that he’s planning to play somewhere else next year," he explained to the Winston-Salem Journal. 

"With those two factors, we just kind of told Tyler: 'Focus on getting healthy; focus on your academics.' And he can start looking to find his next home. It was not a discipline issue."

Tyler's trying to make it as a male model, but also works in construction.

The 6'2" stud has a profile with Next Model Management, and also has representation in New York City and Chicago. 

Though we can’t follow Tyler on social media at the moment, his Instagram page — which Reality Steve revealed to be filled with professional modeling shots — accumulated over 4,500 followers before his stint on The Bachelorette.

Tyler also claims to be a "custom home builder," whatever that means. It reminds us of Arie Luyendyk Jr.’s duel career as a race car driver/real estate agent.

What is Tyler Cameron’s Instagram?

You can follow the Florida native at @tylercameron3, though he probably won’t make his Instagram public again until right before the season premieres. We have a feeling Tyler’s going to rack up hundreds of thousands of followers in no time. 

On The Bachelorette’s Facebook page, his photo is one of the most liked images in the bunch. "He can have all my roses if we[’re] only going by looks. Hopefully he’s honest, sweet and devoted to opening up and finding love," one fan wrote underneath his picture.

Another individual, who appears to know Tyler’s family, commented, "[He’s] the best man on the show. [A] really good person very talented and was brought up with very caring and loving parents. [His] mother has devoted her whole entire life [to] him."

How far does Tyler Cameron make it on The Bachelorette? (SPOILERS)

Though Hannah’s season hasn’t finished shooting, we know for a fact that Tyler makes it to the Final 4. The former Miss Alabama was spotted filming a hometown date in Jupiter on April 22. 

According to pictures provided by Reality Steve, the duo went snorkeling and visited a popular eatery called Square Grouper Tiki Bar. 

Though we don’t know for sure whether Tyler makes it to the Fantasy Suites, Reality Steve claims that based on what he’s heard all season, the former footballer will get a spot in the Top 3. 

The other contestants in Hannah's Final 4 include Luke Parker, Jed Wyatt, and Peter Weber.

Watch the premiere of The Bachelorette on May 13 at 8 p.m. ET on ABC.
