50 most popular women in the world in 2020

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

In this day and age, women have risen to fight for equality in every sphere of life. As much as equality is yet to be achieved, great milestones have been made, and this is thanks to the contribution of popular women. In every sector starting from sports to entertainment and even politics, we have women who have made it through individual effort. Such women deserve to be celebrated for leading the way and opening new possibilities for others. Who are the top 50 most popular women in the world as of the year 2020?

There are many women who have made their mark in this world earning them global fame. A majority of women in this list are in the entertainment industry as musicians or sportswomen. However, there are a couple of other powerful women in politics and activism that have become role models around the world. The most popular women in the world have championed the rights of women to have a society where they are treated equally.

Most popular women in the world

Here are the top 50 popular women in the world:

1. Adele

Adele is a singer and songwriter who is one of the world’s best-selling music artists. The ‘Hello’ singer has had a successful music career with many awards to her name. She has also been named by Time magazine as one of the most influential people in the world twice in 2012 and 2016. Her 2016-2017 tour broke attendance records in several countries including the UK, Australia, and the US.

2. Alicia Keys

Alicia Keys was the host of the 2020 Grammy Awards which confirms her status as one of the most famous women in the world. The American musician has been on the music scene from the age of 15 meaning she has entertained us for over a decade. She has also won multiple awards such as Grammys and is a music icon. She also engages in activism and humanitarian work making her a role model for many.

3. Angela Merkel

Angela Dorothea Merkel is the chancellor of Germany and she has served in that position since 2005 establishing herself as a pioneer among famous female leaders. The German politician is considered a pillar of strength for women who want to venture into politics. She was the leader of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU) from 2000 to 2018 when she resigned as she looks forward to retirement in 2021. As the de facto leader of the European Union, she is the most powerful woman in the world.

4. Angelina Jolie

Angelina Jolie is one of the top famous American women in the world. Her talents on screen have earned her awards such as Academy Award and three Golden Globe Awards. She is among the top highest-earning actresses in Hollywood. Apart from acting, she is a director, screenwriter, and producer. She is said to be one of the most influential and powerful people not only in America but also in the world.

5. Ariana Grande

She is an American singer and actress who hails from Boca Raton, Florida. She gained fame from the Nickelodeon television series titled Victorious between 2010 and 2013, and in its spin-off, Sam & Cat from 2013 to 2014. She then launched a music career that has been nothing but spectacular.

She has won a Grammy Award for Best Pop Vocal Album, a Brit Award, two Billboard Music Awards, three American Music Awards, and seven Guinness World Records. With more than 40 billion streams on music platforms such as Spotify, YouTube and Apple Music, she is definitely one of the most popular women today.

6. Avril Lavigne

Avril Lavigne is a Canadian-French singer, songwriter, and actress who also embodies the importance of music and arts. She pursued her dreams right from a young age and had a contract worth more than $2 million by the age of 16. The queen of pop-punk is a pioneer for female-driven punk-influenced pop music. She is the third best-selling Canadian female artist behind icons Celine Dion and Shania Twain.

7. Beyonce Knowles

With 139 million followers on Instagram, Beyonce ranks among the most popular women to ever walk the earth. She is an American singer who has excelled and led the industry for a very long time and has been a role model for other female icons.

Among other things, she is a songwriter, record producer, actress, and philanthropist. She has enjoyed fame from the 1990s which is why she is considered very influential today. Another thing making her famous is her high profile marriage to fellow celebrity Jay Z.

8. Britney Spears

Britney Spears is yet another American who makes it to the list. She is a singer, songwriter, dancer, and actress whose work has made her world-famous. Although she had been featuring in stage production and television series, her big break came when she signed a contract with Jive Records in 1997.

Her two albums in 1999 and 2000 made her the best-selling teenage artist of all time and she earned the nickname ‘Princess of Pop.’ She has produced chart-topping music since then earning many awards and accolades in the process.

10. Cardi B

Cardi B is an American rapper, songwriter, TV personality, and actress. She has not been famous for long like other names in this list but she has made headlines for herself within a short time. She became an internet celebrity only between 2015 and 2017 after posting viral videos on Vine. Since then, she has released hit after hit.

14. Ellen DeGeneres

She is also hailed for coming out publicly as a gay person. Her flourishing career is highlighted by a number of awards and the fact that she has hosted the Academy Awards, Grammy Awards, and Primetime Emmys. As a TV personality, she is without a doubt one of the most influential women.

Elena Jane Golding is an English singer and songwriter. Her first album debuted in 2009 and she went on to win a number of awards. 16. Emma Watson

Emma Watson is an English actress, model, and activist. This is the dream of every young girl to be a model and a flourishing career. She was trained as an actress at the Stagecoach Theatre Arts in Oxford.

Her fame came when she was featured in the Harry Potter series as Hermione Granger. She has also acted in Ballet Shoes as well as lending her voice for The Tale of Despereaux. Since then, she has worked on a number of films the latest ones being Little Women (2019), Beauty and the Beast (2017) and The Circle (2017).

17. Eva Longoria

Eva Jacqueline Longoria Bastón wears many hats which include actress, producer, businesswoman, activist, and director. The American appeared on several guest roles in TV series before eventually getting recognized for playing Isabella Braña on a soap titled The Young and the Restless that used to air on CBS. 18. Greta Thunberg

Greta Thunberg is an environmental activist on climate change who inspired young people around the world to demand radical changes in protecting our planet. 19. Halle Berry

The American Halle Berry is an actress famously known from her performance in 2001’s romantic drama film Monster's Ball for which she won the Academy Award for Best Actress. She became the first woman of African American descent to win the award. She is also a model who once contested in the Miss USA pageant and Miss World. She has been active as an actress since 1989 to date and therefore making her known all over the world.

21. Hilary Clinton

Hillary Diane Rodham Clinton almost became the first female US President only to be defeated in the electoral vote by Trump even though she won the popular vote. 22. Ivanka Trump

27. Kesha

28. Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian is undoubtedly one of the most popular women in the world. The American media personality is also a businesswoman, socialite, model, and actress. She gained fame from her friendship with Paris Hilton and was also her stylist.

31. Kylie Minogue

Kylie Ann Minogue is an Australian singer, songwriter, and actress. She is an icon both in the music and fashion industry and has constantly reinvented herself. The style icon also happens to be the highest-selling female Australian artist of all time.

Her career began as an actress in an Australian soap opera and then moved into the UK to venture into music. She has been successful throughout her career and has many awards to show for it.

32. Lady Gaga

Born Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta, Lady Gaga is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. As a child, she used to sing at open mic nights and often acted in school plays. She dropped out of school to focus on music and established herself as a pop icon.

33. Lana Del Rey

Lana Del Rey is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. She is credited for producing music with stylized cinematic quality. She has greatly influenced pop culture with themes such as tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia.

34. Madonna

Madonna is an iconic singer and songwriter born in America. She is also an actress and businesswoman. The "Queen of Pop" earned the title from her mastery in songwriting and her imagery-rich music videos. She has influenced many artists and has fans all over the world.

35. Mariah Carey

The vocally talented musician is also a songwriter, actress, record producer, and businesswoman. She is known for creating award-winning love songs. She has had unprecedented success as an artist. During the 1990s, she was the most successful artist in the US and is among the best-selling music artists in the entire world.

36. Marilyn Monroe

The American actress, model, and singer is world-famous for playing comedic "blonde bombshell" characters thus making her a sex symbol. She helped change attitudes towards sexuality. Even though she died in 1962, she is still one of the popular women today.

37. Megan Fox

The American actress and model has enjoyed fame since 2001 when she started acting. Her long career as an actress has seen her grow on film and fame as well. Considered a sex symbol, Megan Fox constantly appears in magazine covers including Maxim, Rolling Stone, and FHM.

38. Meghan Markle

Meghan Markle is the Duchess of Sussex and that alone is enough to make her famous. However, she was even famous before marrying into the royal family and enjoyed celebrity status as an actress. She began featuring in minor roles in TV series and films while still in school but her big break was in the 2011’s Suits that was aired for seven years. She has voiced issues of gender inequality and is an inspiration to many feminists around the world.

39. Melinda Gates

A former general manager at Microsoft, Melinda Gates is now a philanthropist whose humanitarian work is acknowledged all over the world. She also happens to be the wife of Bill Gates who is one of the richest men on the planet. Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation is the largest private charitable organization in the entire world. It is no doubt that she is among the most popular women in the world.

40. Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She has also engaged in noble courses as a philanthropist donating large amounts of money for charity purposes. As a singer, she is known to be flexible spanning various styles such as pop, country-pop, and hip hop. Her lifestyle has elicited strong criticisms at times but the controversies also bring media coverage making her famous as a result.

41. Michelle Obama

Michelle Obama is an influential personality who is the former first lady of the United States. Before that, she used to work as a lawyer, university administrator, and writer. She is the wife of the 44th President of the United States, Barack Obama. She made history as the first African American to be the First Lady of the US.

42. Nancy Pelosi

The American politician Nancy Pelosi is a beacon of hope that women can make it in leadership. She is one of the most popular women in the world. The Democratic Party leader currently serves as Speaker of the United States House of Representatives making her the first woman to hold that position. This also makes her the highest-ranking female elected official in the history of the US.

43. Nicki Minaj

Nicki Minaj is an award-winning American rapper and pop singer. She entered the public scene in 2007 when she released mixtapes Playtime Is Over (2007), Sucka Free (2008), and Beam Me Up Scotty (2009). Although she has been very successful as a rapper, she also ventured into film with her debut being the 2012 animated film Ice Age: Continental Drift. Named in the 2016 Time 100 list, she is still very influential in and outside music.

44. Oprah Winfrey

The American media executive is probably among the top 10 most famous women in the world. She is a talk show host, an actress, a TV producer, and a philanthropist. Her highly emotional talks on The Oprah Winfrey Show are followed by viewers from all over the world.

She was declared the richest African American of the 20th century and also ranks as the greatest African American philanthropist in history. Many female pioneers championing for the rights of women identify with her and the role she has played to make the world a better place.

45. Priyanka Chopra

Priyanka Chopra Jonas is an Indian actress, singer, film producer, and model. She is also the winner of the Miss World 2000 beauty pageant. She is ranked among the highest-paid celebrities in India. She has many awards and was named by Time to be one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2016. She has also been named as one of the world’s most powerful women by Forbes.

46. Queen Elizabeth II

This is as high as you can go on the list of powerful women in the world. It is a lifetime of fame for Queen Elizabeth as she has been the leader of the United Kingdom and other Commonwealth realms. Before she became queen, she served in the Auxiliary Territorial Service during World War II. She took over when her father died in 1952 and has remained at the throne since then.

47. Rihanna

Robyn Rihanna Fenty is a Barbadian singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur who is famous all over the world. She has received recognition for being able to adapt to several musical styles and remaining relevant in the music industry for a very long time. She is also a sex symbol whose beauty tips are emulated across the globe. She has won many awards for her music and has also tried acting making her one of the most popular women on earth.

48. Selena Gomez

For a long time, Selena Gomez was the most followed individual on Instagram. The American singer is also an accomplished actress, television producer, and songwriter. She has been in the public limelight from a tender age when she used to in the children's television series Barney & Friends (2002–2004).

She has won awards both as an actor and a singer and ranks high as an influential celebrity. She was also constantly in the headlines when she was dating fellow singer Justin Bieber and when the two broke up fans couldn’t help but be sad because they thought they were meant for each other.

49. Serena Williams

One of the greatest sportswomen of all time, Serena Williams is a global icon and role model for other women. She is an American tennis player who has been a world No. 1 in the sport for many years. Her consistency has made her a legend and today she ranks as one of the most popular women in the world. Through her excellence in the tennis court, she is one of the famous positive female role models today.

50. Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is an American singer and songwriter. Most of her music is made from narrative songs on themes from her personal life. This technique has received widespread media coverage as she is criticized and praised in equal measure.

She was signed by Sony/ATV Music publishing house when she was 14 years old. Her eponymous debut album was the longest-charting album in the United States in the 2000s. She has many accolades to her name and has been praised for writing most of her songs.

In a world where women face inequality and discrimination from a patriarchal society, it is difficult for women to achieve their dreams and compete with men. That said, it would be an injustice to imply that women have not risen to the occasion and curved their destinies. There are many women who have excelled in different spheres of life and deserve recognition for it. A list of 50 most popular women in the world does not do justice to the numbers of women who are leaders in different ways but it surely is a place to start.
