Keilly Alonso - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Working alongside her brother, she is best known for being the founder of the YouTube channel Keilly and Kendry. Together, they would create slime content, do challenges and record vlogs. She also has her own self-titled channel. Her "OPENING CHRISTMAS PRESENTS 2019 :KEILLY AND KENDRY" video has received over 220 thousand views since it came out in April of 2020. 

Before Fame

She was the lone member of the channel at first, posting solo videos such as "Justice Haul (first Video)" and "Slime Smoothie." The channel was called Keilly Slays at that time. 

Trivia

Her and her brother's channel would accrue more than 300,000 subscribers. Her keillyalon Instagram account has over 170,000 followers and has featured multiple pictures of her posing at the beach. 

Family Life

She would co-run her popular YouTube channel with her brother Kendry. They have an older brother named Kevin and their mom's name is Yelany. Their cousins run the Sister Forever channel.

Associated With

She said in a Q&A video that one of her favorite Team 10 members is Chad Tepper.
