Nigerians React As Bride Refuses To Kiss Her Groom During Their Wedding

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, July 7, 2024

A video showing the moment a Nigerian bride refused to accept a kiss offering from her groom during their wedding has surfaced on social media.

In the video that has become a growing sensation, the young bride was seen frowning while the groom tried to place a kiss

It is alleged that the young bride was forced into the union, hence her show of rejection during her white wedding to the groom who looked displeased after she turned down his kiss offering.

The video got social media reeling with reactions as many social media users expressed worry over the young bride.
