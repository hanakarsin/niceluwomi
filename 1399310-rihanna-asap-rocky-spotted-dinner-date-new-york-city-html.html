Rihanna and ASAP Rocky spotted at dinner date in New York City

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

- Rihanna and ASAP Rocky had dinner together at an Italian restaurant in New York City this week

- The duo and a couple of their friends spent two hours in the popular restaurant

- A source shared that Riri and the rapper entered the restaurant through different doors

Rihanna and ASAP Rocky were once again spotted together on a dinner date in New York City. The two lovebirds enjoyed the date night with a couple of friends on Monday, January 18.

According to reports, the famous musicians ate at the popular Italian restaurant, Emilio Ballato. A source shared that the celebrity couple arrived separately to the venue.

The source said Riri arrived with a couple of her friends and met her rapper boyfriend there. They also entered the restaurant through different doors.

The source told E! News that the We Found Love hitmaker and the Praise the Lord rapper tried their best to be discreet on the night. The insider explained that Rocky entered behind his bodyguard and sneaked inside quickly.
