Nyamira: Residents Gatecrash DG James Gesami's Party, Scramble For Food

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, July 18, 2024
  • James Gesami had hosted the area governor Amos Nyaribo to a party to celebrate his swearing-in
  • However, several residents who had milled around Gesami's homestead sneaked in through the thorny fence
  • The residents scrambled for food with some eating directly from Sufurias and hotpots

A ceremony organised by Nyamira deputy governor James Gesami at his home in Gianchore turned chaotic after residents gatecrashed and scrambled for food.

Gesami had hosted the area governor Amos Nyaribo to a party to celebrate his swearing-in, an exercise that had faced hurdles for seven months.

In the Tuesday, August 17 incident, several residents who had milled around Gesami's homestead despite the beefed-up security sneaked in through the thorny fence.

The hungry residents, whose sole aim was to get food, took over the operations from the outside catering team that had been tasked to feed the attendees.

After being satisfied, the residents deserted leaving behind empty dishes scattered in the compound.
