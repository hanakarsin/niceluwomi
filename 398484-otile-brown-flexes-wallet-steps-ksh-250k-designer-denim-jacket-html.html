Otile Brown flexes his wallet as he steps out in KSh 250k designer denim jacket

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, March 7, 2024

- If you have been following Otile, then you should know the number of hits he brags of on different online streaming sites

- TUKO.co.ke understands the streaming sites pays the singer a lot of money in form of revenue for his music

- Many people could not believe the singer can splash a whole KSh 250,000 on a simple denim jacket

The year 2020 was one of the worst for many people including local celebrities lke musicians, socialites and actors.

Due to coronavirus safety guidelines, many events could not take place which meant great loss not only for the artistes but also the events organisers.

Since coronavirus disrupted the economy, many artistes were rendered broke with no shows and other deals to cash in on.

However, there are some artistes who were equally affected by the pandemic but reaped big in other avenues such as online music streaming revenue.

Sensational Kenyan singer Otile Brown happens to be among the few East African musicians who reaped big from their music in 2020.

While many artists focused on getting shows so they could get money to survive during the pandemic, Otile and others like him invested more on getting their products on streaming platforms to multiply their revenue.

The musician recently confirmed to Kenyans that he indeed made a lot of money after stepping out in an expensive jacket.

Otile, on Thursday, December 31, rocked a KSh 250,000 Guccy denim jacket which left netizens green with envy.

Taking to Instagram, the Aiyana hitmaker shared a photo of himself flaunting his wear whose cost is equal to a plot of land in Kamulu but did not put any caption on it.

Many who know fashion camped on the comments section and marvelled at his jacket.

TUKO.co.ke did a quick online research on the price of Otile's denim jacket and was shocked to realise some online stores such as Monti Boutique sell the jacket at KSh 250,000.

While the price shook some people, it did not come as a surprise to many who know how much the singer is worth.

It should be noted the singer rolls in a top of the range Mercedes Benz.
