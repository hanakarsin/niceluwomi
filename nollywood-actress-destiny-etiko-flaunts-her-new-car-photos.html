Nollywood Actress, Destiny Etiko Flaunts Her New Car (Photos)

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Popular Nollywood actress, Destiny Etiko, has just bought a brand new Toyota Land Cruiser Prado for herself and has taken to social media to share the pictures.

As seen from the pictures she shared on her Instagram page, the actress got the car a customized “Drama Doll” number plate.

Sharing the pictures, the 31-year-old Enugu born actress added the caption: “An extra baby added to my garage. MULTIPLE INCREASE ONLY. Thank your Lord. WWUDD.”

See more photos of the new car.

Meanwhile, Nigerian hip-hop singer and songwriter, David Adeleke, better known as Davido will be performing live at the birthday ceremony of Primate Elijah Ayodele, the leader of INRI Evangelical spiritual church.

The renowned prophet disclosed during a chat with newsmen that he had spoken with the singer to come and perform at his church.

Naija News understands that Ayodele invited Davido and his crew to grace his birthday ceremony on February 14, 2021.

The prophet revealed that the singer will not just be a guest but will be leading songs at the birthday ceremony which is scheduled to hold at his Church.
