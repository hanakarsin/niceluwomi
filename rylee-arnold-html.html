Rylee Arnold - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, February 2, 2024

Originally known as the sister of dancer Lindsay Arnold from ABC's Dancing with the Stars. She went on to star on Dancing with the Stars Junior in October 2018.

Before Fame

Like her sister, she grew up dancing from a very young age.

Trivia

She made the finals at the WDC World Championship in Paris in 2014. She and her sisters run the collaborative YouTube channel The Arnold Sisters.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Provo, Utah the youngest of four sisters. Apart from Lindsay, her other two sisters are named Jensen and Brynley. She has been romantically linked to Truman Burningham. 

Associated With

She's danced with Justin Bieber.
