Are Mutt Lange and Marie-Anne still together? Everything you should know

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Celebrity couples attract tremendous publicity and curiosity into their personal lives. Mutt Lange and Marie-Anne are no exception. The two were part of an incredible saga that included Mutt’s divorce from renowned singer Shania Twain, Shania’s marriage to Marie-Anne’s ex-husband, and a broken friendship between Shania and Marie-Anne.

Mutt Lange and Marie-Anne hit the headlines some years back when their secret relationship marked the beginning of a series of events involving betrayal, divorces, and new marriages.

Who are Mutt Lange and Marie-Anne?

Robert John 'Mutt' Lange is a Zambian-born South African record producer and songwriter. Lange was born in November 1948 in Zambia but was raised in South Africa. He has mixed ethnicity from his German mother and South African father.

Mutt was introduced to record production in his early years and has since worked with numerous artists including Britney Spears, Maroon 5, Bryan Adams, Michael Bolton, and Lady Gaga. His work has earned him numerous Grammy Awards.

Marie-Anne Thiébaud, on the other hand, was known by many as Shania Twain's best friend and long-time secretary. She was a close friend of the family and worked as Shania's assistant. She managed the renowned singer’s 46-room palace in Switzerland.

Marie-Ann would also help with Shania's son whenever the country music star needed to travel. Other details of her background have remained undisclosed. The two also attended social events together.

Mutt Lange and Shania Twain

Mutt reached out to Canadian songstress Shania after listening to her debut self-titled album, Shania Twain (1993). Lange was interested in co-writing and producing her music. They kept in touch through the phone and met six months after the initial contact.

Shortly after meeting, Mutt and Shania got into a romantic relationship and tied the knot on December 28, 1993. In August 2001, the couple were blessed with a son named Eja (pronounced 'Asia').

The couple was in a seemingly stable union until 2008 when things began to change. Robert Lange and Twain divorced officially on June 9, 2010.

Marie-Anne Thiebaud's interview

When rumours of Lange’s affair began to surface, Marie-Anne appeared in an interview with the People Magazine and vehemently denied the allegations. She asked the interviewer not to believe everything he heard in the press.

Mutt Lange's affair
