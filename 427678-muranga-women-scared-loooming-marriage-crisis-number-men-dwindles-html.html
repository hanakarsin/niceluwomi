Murang'a: Women Scared of Looming Marriage Crisis as Number of Men Dwindles

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Wednesday, August 21, 2024
  • Murang'a women organisations said the county currently has a deficit of 8,729 men and any more deficiency will affect marriages
  • The women attributed the reduction to increased imprisonment and mob lynching of young men
  • Since Uhuru ordered a crackdown on illicit brew in the Central region, 30,000 youths have died, 40% of them being from Murang'a

There is a scare of a marriage crisis in Murang'a if the number of men will continue dwindling in the county.

Women organisations have since raised concerns over the reduction in the number of men in the county, which they attributed to increased imprisonment and mob lynching of young men.

As reported by Nation, the women said the county currently has a deficit of 8,729 men and any more deficiency will affect the founding of new families.

Illicit brew

Since July 2015, when President Uhuru Kenyatta ordered a crackdown on illicit brew in the Central region, 30,000 youths have died, 40% of them being from Murang'a.

Interior PS Karanja Kibicho disclosed this in Thika while launching the Rapid Results Initiative (RRI) against narcotics and illicit brews.

Weighing in on the matter, Mixson Warui, the Murang'a Young Professionals Action Network chair, blamed the Directorate of Criminal Investigations for lacking a structured way to deal with organised crimes.

This, Warui said, has led to an increase in illicit brews and vigilante cartels that continue to threaten the lives of the male population in the Mwangi wa Iria led county.

Woman searching for man

In other news, a desperate woman took to the streets looking for a husband, saying age was not on her side.

In a YouTube video, the woman was seen carrying a placard in Buza, Dar es Salaam indicating the type of man she is looking for.

Speaking to a Tanzanian YouTuber, the woman said she will pay her dowry and claimed she is still a virgin.

She also divulged that she bought the wedding dress for KSh 142,000.
