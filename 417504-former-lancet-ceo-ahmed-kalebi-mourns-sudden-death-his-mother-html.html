Former Lancet CEO Ahmed Kalebi Mourns Sudden Death of His Mother

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, July 25, 2024
  • Former Lancet CEO Ahmed Kalebi lost his mother on Thursday, July 1, shortly after celebrating his nephew's birthday
  • Kalebi said his mother collapsed as they walked towards his car and his efforts to perform a CPR on her bore no fruits
  • According to him, it was his mother's prayer that when her time comes to exist the world, she would do so peacefully without being sickly
  • She will be buried on Friday, July 2, at the Kibra Muslim Cemetery

Retired Lancet CEO Ahmed Kalebi is mourning following the sudden demise of his mother Hajjat Maalim Zuhura Sebit Rajab, who died on Thursday, July 1, night.

Zuhura died shortly after spending quality time with her family members, where they shared an evening meal at her granddaughter's place.

Announcing the sad news on his social media account, Kalebi said his mother collapsed while they were walking back to the car after attending his nephew's birthday.

The former CEO and founder of Lancet said it was his mother's wish to have a peaceful death, and he was grateful that her prayers had been perfectly answered.

"Mom has always been reiterating her wish - even as late as last Sunday while talking to my sisters- that when her times comes, she prays to have a swift death without being sickly or incapacitated," tweeted Kalebi.

Collapsing suddenly

Zuhura collapsed while in the company of Kalebi, his wife and two sons, and his frantic efforts to perform CPR on her bore no fruits as she was pronounced dead ten minutes later.

Kalebi also noted that the recent turn of events was totally unexpected as his mother had been otherwise well with a clean bill of health from recent medical checkups.

"The turn of events happened suddenly after a busy and joyful night out at my daughter’s for Thursday iftar and a stop at my nephew’s birthday," he wrote.

"She collapsed around 09:10pm when we were walking to the car with my wife, all my boys and myself; she died almost immediately in our hands," he added.

Messages of condolence

Her body lies at Kalebi's maternal grandmother's place in Kambi Muru, Kibra and will be buried on Friday, July 2, at the Kibra Muslim Cemetery immediately after prayers are performed.

Friends shared their condolences messages while offering words of encouragement and prayers to the family as they mourned.

Irene Chebet wrote:

"My sincere condolence Ahmed Kalebi. May Allah grant you the peace that you need at this painful moment!"

Peter Tosh wrote:

"Peace be with you Doc as you mourn the sudden death of your dear Mama."

Kalebi's retirement

On March 10, Kalebi made a personal statement announcing his retirement as group managing director, CEO and chief consultant pathologist of Lancet Kenya starting April 2021.

“The retirement is more of a much-deserved sabbatical/career break that I planned to step down at 45 years of age committing myself to thereafter dedicate more time to my family and also to pursue various personal interests and endeavours including a PhD as well as future ventures,” he wrote.

The reactions to his announcement were swift with many terming it bold, especially at 45 when many Kenyans are hoping to hit the prime of their careers.

Many social media users complimented Kalebi for his workaholic traits that helped transform Lancet into one of the leading medical diagnostic laboratories in the region.
