Makini School - fees structure, location, contacts

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, August 3, 2024

In February 1978, Makini School was founded. Then, it was called the Riara Gardens Academy. It started as a nursery school with a total of 8 children. The structure that served as the school was a colonial bungalow where the late Dr. Okelo and Dr. Mary Okelo, the founders of the school, lived. The school then grew with an increasing number of students. In 1980, another plot was acquired on the other side Kirichwa river. The following year, 1981, the school was renamed to Makini Schools.

Makini School has evolved over the years to become one of the best learning institutions in Kenya. The school offers the Kenyan curriculum with International standards, thus allowing students to reach their maximum potential. In 2018, Makini School was acquired by Scholé International whose management practices bring global practices with the intention of making Makini a world class institution.

As a parent, you can choose to take your child to any of the Makini School campuses across the country. But first, understanding what is expected of you is a must. Both the fees structure, location, and contacts of the school will help you secure admission for your child into Makini School.

Makini School mission and vision

As a parent, the mission and the vision of a school is a strong determiner when choosing the best school for your child as it gives you a feel of what a school is all about. For Makini School, these are her mission and vision:

Mission

Makini will actively encourage and facilitate: The formation of each student’s moral character, unique skills, capabilities and talents; the creation of a strong communal ethos of social responsibility; and the achievement of the highest possible academic results.

Vision

Makini’s vision is to provide quality education at affordable cost and to create centres of excellence in which each child will develop: the highest possible moral, academic, cultural and sporting standards; a wide range of interests, capacities and talents; a strong sense of social responsibility and carry these values into adult life.

Makini School location

There are several Makini School locations across Kenya. These are:

  • Makini Ngong Road Campus (Pre & Primary School)
  • Makini Junior Academy, State House Avenue (Preschool & Lower Primary)
  • Ngong Road Campus (High school)
  • Makini Preschool and Lower Primary, Migosi, Kisumu
  • Makini Kisumu City Campus
  • Makini Kibos Primary & High School (2021)
