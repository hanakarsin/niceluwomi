15 black celebrities with curly hair who look great in 2021

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, May 6, 2024

The famous phrase, ''I am not my hair,'' means that there is much more to a person than their hair and that the way one chooses to wear their hair should not define them. However, black celebrities with curly hair are a force to reckon with in the beauty and fashion world because they express their personalities through their beautiful and unique strands.

Hair is an expression of an individual's style and personality. For most individuals, hair should make a bold statement of who they are even before saying a word. The beauty of hair is that you can wear it in whatever style or colour, so several public figures are always trying new and trendy looks. So, who are these black celebrities with curly hair?

Black celebrities with curly hair

Whether short or long, hair remains one of the most significant fashion statements that a celebrity can make. Most of these famous people go to great lengths to achieve that perfect look. However, a good number of them have embraced curls as a stylish way to rock their hair. Here is a list of black celebrities with frizzy hair in 2021:

1. Gabrielle Union-Wade

Gabrielle Union is an American actress famous for movies like Daddy's Little Girls (2007), Think Like a Man (2012), and Think Like a Man Too (2014). The 49-year-old is one of the famous actresses with dark curly hair, and she enjoys styling it in different ways.

The Hollywood star recently embraced short hair and has received numerous compliments from her fans and celebrity friends. Gabrielle is married to Dwayne Wade. Unknown to many, Union once hated her natural hair but now gladly embraces its strength and diversity.

2. Taraji P. Henson

Taraji Penda Henson is an American actress born on September 11, 1970. This gorgeous entertainer is one of the black actresses with natural curly hair. Taraji has appeared in films like Hidden Figures (2016) and Acrimony (2018).

Henson runs a natural hair products line, TPH By Taraji, whose goal is to create the optimal environment for healthy hair, whatever the style. Her company uses natural products like apple cider vinegar and avocado oil that nourish hair in incredible ways.

3. Sanaa Lathan

Sanaa is an American actress and voice artist born on September 19, 1971. She has appeared in numerous films, including The Perfect Guy (2015) and Nappily Ever After (2019). Lathan is one of the light-skin girls with curly hair, although she rarely rocks her natural strands.

The voice actress made headlines when she chopped off her mane during the filming of Nappily Ever After. However, her strands have grown back, and she is excited to show off her natural hair. She shared that healthy regrowth is a result of protective styles, braids and almost no heat.

4. Zendaya

Zendaya, whose full name is Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman, was born in Oakland, California, on September 1, 1996. The American singer and actress has mixed ethnicity, which could explain her gorgeous strands.

The filmmaker is bold and daring, rocking numerous hairstyles to different red carpet events. Zendaya also loves to experiment with different hair colours. The actress shares that continuous heat-styling ruined her hair when she was filming Disney's Shake it Up. However, she has learned to take proper care of her tresses and now confidently rocks her beautiful curly hair.

5. Da Real BB Judy

Jesseca Dupart, whose stage name is Da Real BB Judy, is an American entrepreneur born on February 12, 1982. Dupart is the founder and CEO of an expansive hair care product line called Kaleidoscope Hair Products, famous for Miracle Drops.

Miracle Drops is a hair and scalp oil product that helps combat dry scalp, aids in hair growth, and offers a natural, lightweight sheen. The oil also removes excessive hair buildup.

6. Yara Shahidi

Yara is an American model, actress, and activist born in 2000 and famous for her role in Black-ish (2020). The 21-year-old is also one of the black celebrities with natural curly hair and has mixed ethnicity from her African-American and Iranian parents.

Yara has described her hair as a literal representation of the different identities that make her who she is. She has stated that she loves her big curls and that they give her confidence to take up whatever space she occupies.

7. Rihanna

Robyn Rihanna Fenty is a Barbadian musician, fashion designer, and businesswoman born on February 20, 1988, in St Michael, Barbados. Besides having numerous hit singles to her name, Rihanna is also the founder of Fenty Beauty.

The singer loves to wear various hairstyles and colours, which accentuate her natural beauty. In addition, she is bold enough to rock even the most daring haircuts and will look good either way.

8. Sevyn Streeter

Sevyn, known fully as Amber Denise "Sevyn" Streeter, is an American singer-songwriter born on July 7, 1986. One of her most famous roles was when she was a member of the girl groups TG4 and RichGirl, where she was known as Se7en.

Sevyn has a long and gorgeous natural mane and is not afraid to chop it off because it will grow back. The singer once damaged her hair due to continuous straightening. However, she has since learnt to maintain it using the right products and a hair care routine.

9. Beyonce

''Queen Bee'' is famous for gracing the red carpet with dashing and daring outfits, but also for her bold hairstyles. Although she loves her brai`ds, wigs, and extensions, Beyonce is one of the black celebrities with natural hair that is curly.

More recently, the 40-year-old singer has embraced a more natural look, and her fans love it. Since her earlier years, Beyonce has always rocked her signature light brown strands, which flow freely to her shoulders.

10. Ciara

Ciara Princess Wilson is an American singer-songwriter, model, and businesswoman born on October 25, 1985, in Fort Hood, Texas. She is famous for her song Level Up (2018).

Ciara recently shared her struggles with her hair, which experienced extensive breakage because of using chemical relaxers when she was younger. However, her hair is now growing back, and she is slowly gaining confidence to rock her natural strands without extensions.

11. Keke Palmer

Keke is an American singer, actress and TV personality. She was born on August 26, 1993, in Harvey, Illinois. She gained popularity when she became the youngest nominee for the Screen Actors Guild's best leading actress honour.

Like many other celebrities, Palmer has also tried out numerous hairstyles, including weaves and faux locs. The actress did not embrace her kinky hair growing up, but she now rocks it with confidence and has accepted it as a beautiful part of who she is.

12. Jessica Williams

Jessica Renee Williams is an American actress, producer, writer, and comedian. The gorgeous 32-year-old also makes it to the list of black celebrities with curly hair. Jessica has recently appeared in the 2017 Netflix comedy movie The Incredible Jessica James.

The actress loves braiding her hair, which resulted from escaping the pain she went through as a child when she had to be hot combed and pressed. However, the TV personality has grown to embrace and rock her natural strands, accepting them as part of her.

13. Kerry Washington

Kerry Marisa Washington is an American actress and producer born on January 31, 1977, in The Bronx, New York City, USA. Some of her work includes Django Unchained (2012), Scandal (2012) and Ray (2004). She is a mother of two.

Kerry loves to experiment using different hairstyles, from loose curls to coils and anything in-between. The actress says that having a daughter has opened up her inner sparkle, which has helped her embrace her natural strands.

14. Maya Jama

Maya Indea Jama is an English model, actress, TV, and radio presenter born on August 14, 1994, in Bristol, United Kingdom. Maya has Somali and Swedish roots, a factor to which many have attributed her stunning looks.

Jama has natural loose curls, and she likes to straighten them out as often as she can. The model maintains her hair using leave-in treatment alongside other natural products, which help keep her hair soft and shiny.
