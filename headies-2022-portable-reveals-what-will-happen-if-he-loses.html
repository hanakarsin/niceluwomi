Portable Reveals What Will Happen If He Loses

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Fast-rising singer, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable, has vowed to unleash havoc if he doesn’t win Headies Awards, which is set to hold on September 4, 2022, in Atlanta Georgia.

Naija News reports that Portable, alongside, Wizkid, Davido and Burna Boy were nominated for the forthcoming Headies award.

Recall, Headies released all of the nominees for the upcoming event on its official Instagram page. The list also had some first timers like Portable, and other fast-rising artists.

Reacting to his nomination, Portable via the micro blogging platform, Twitter, vowed to unleash mayhem with his boys if he doesn’t win the category he was nominated for.

He wrote; ”If I no win any Headies award, this country, no go contain us, me and my boys go riot”
