Nigerians React As Peter Obi Visits Redemption City

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Nigerians have reacted to the visit of the Labour Party Presidential Candidate, Peter Obi, to the Redemption City for the first time.

Naija News earlier reported that the LP presidential flagbearer attended the 70th annual convention of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) on Friday.

The announcer, while welcoming Obi to the convention on Friday, said, “We welcome to the Redemption Camp for the first time Mr Peter Obi.”

The introduction of the former Governor of Anambra was greeted with loud cheers from the audience at the convention.

Recall that Obi recently got the same reception at the Dunamis International Gospel Centre vigil in Abuja, on the 29th of July.

The entire congregation went agog after the wife of General Overseer Paul Enenche, Becky introduced the presidential candidate.

Becky expressed excitement about having Obi in their auditorium.

Following the latest development, some Nigerians have taken to the micro-blogging platform, Twitter to express their views on the matter.
