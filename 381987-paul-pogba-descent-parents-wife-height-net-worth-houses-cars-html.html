Paul Pogba: descent, parents, wife, height, net worth, houses, cars

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Who is Paul Pogba? He is one of the top professional footballers, best known for his flamboyant personality – both on and off the field. From his impressive attacking skills to his eye-catching hairstyles, he never fails to catch the attention of fans and critics.

From his African roots to being a world champion with the French national team, Paul Pogba's life is an inspiration to many. But who is Pogba off the field? Unfortunately, not many people know about him when he is not playing.

Profile summary

  • Name: Paul Labile Pogba
  • Date of birth: 15th March 1993
  • Age: 27 years of 2020
  • Place of birth: Lagny-Sur-Marne, France
  • Profession: Professional footballer
  • Partner: Marlia Zulay

Early life

Pogba was born on 15th March 1993 as Paul Labile Pogba. He was born at Lagny-Sur-Marne in France to Yeo Moriba and Fassoue Antoine. At the time, his parents had migrated to France in search of a better life; his mother being Congolese, and his father Guinean.

As a result, Pogba was able to acquire French citizenship. Before migrating, his parents had left three elder sons in Conakry, Guinea, who would join the family in France later.

Growing up, Pogba did not have a strong relationship with his father, given that he was a distant person. However, he formed a close relationship with his mother, who has been one of his strongest supporters throughout his career.

From an early age, Pogba was passionate about football. After picking the sport as a hobby while in school, he decided to make a career out of it. At just six years old, he joined US Roissy-en-Brie, a football club that was a short distance from his home. He played in the club for seven years, during which time he gained outstanding skills, becoming a talented young player.

Pogba's passion for football spilled to his brothers, who soon became addicted to the sport. With strong support from their mother, they all grew up to become professional football players.

Career

Paul Pogba's journey to becoming a professional footballer started when he was very young. After playing for US Roissy-en-Brie for seven years, he moved to US Torcy. His talent on the field earned him captainship of the team at just 13 years. Despite being a teenager, his star was shining bright, and soon afterwards he joined Le Havre.

Pogba proved himself to be an invaluable asset to the club several times. At one time, he captained the under-16 team to the final stages of the domestic league. The exposure and his impressive talent on the field saw him gain the attention of several European clubs.

Aside from club football, Pogba also established himself as a reliable player for France’s youth teams. He started playing for his country by appearing for the under-16 France national team. He was also called up for the under-20 side, where he captained them to winning the 2013 FIFA under-20 world cup.

Pogba’s big break in professional football came in 2009 when he transferred to the English Premier League side, Manchester United. At the time, he was just 16 years old; relatively young, but already proven to be an extremely talented player.

Unfortunately, despite the big move, Pogba spent a frustrating two seasons at United. After receiving very little playtime, he moved to the Italian Serie-A side, Juventus. There, he helped the club to several events such as:

  • Clinching four uninterrupted Serie-A league wins
  • Clinching Coppa Italia and Supercoppa Italia titles
  • Reaching the 2015 UEFA Champions League Finals

In 2016, Pogba returned to his old club, Manchester United. The move resulted in a massive, record transfer fee of £89.3 million, making him the most expensive footballer at the time.

Following his return to United, Pogba became instrumental in several of the club’s achievements. For example, he helped them win the Europa League cup as well as the League Cup (Carabao Cup). He also continued to appear for the French national team with impressive results. In 2018, was part of the Les Bleus team that won the World Cup final against Croatia.

Net worth and earnings

Ever wondered how much Pogba is worth? Like most professional footballers, particularly those appearing for big clubs, he earns quite a considerable sum of money. Paul Pogba net worth is estimated to be around $125 million.

How about earnings? How much is Paul Pogba salary? After his record transfer to Manchester United, Pogba became one of the highest-paid players in the world. Currently, his contract sees him take home a salary of £290,000 per month, which translates into £15,080,000 a year. Therefore, he is one of Manchester United's big earners.

Aside from the base salary, Pogba also rakes in thousands of pounds in bonuses every season. For example, he receives a bonus of £50,000 for every goal scored and a further £20,000 for every assist. Additionally, he gets a loyalty bonus every season for staying at the club, which amounted to £3.0 million in 2019.

On top of the bonuses, Pogba, being a recognized personality and influencer worldwide, also earns huge from endorsement deals. Currently, his most lucrative endorsement is with sports company Adidas, which is worth £31 million for ten years.

Personal life and family

Who is Paul Pogba off the field? The footballer has an exciting life away from the 90 minutes, or so, he appears in front of the camera.

Parents

If there is one person Pogba credits with his successful career, it is his mother. When he was just two years old, Yeo Moriba separated from her husband, leaving her with the challenging task of raising three sons.

Raising one son is hard enough, but when it is three boys who have an interest in professional football, it is almost impossible. Nonetheless, she handled the job correctly, and Pogba and his brothers usually refer to her as a supermom.

As for his father, Pogba did not have such a close relationship with him, especially after the separation. Nonetheless, they maintained a bond, and he was even at the stands during the Euro 16. Sadly, the elder Pogba passed away in 2017 after being ill for quite some time.

Siblings

Pogba has two brothers: Peile Florentin Pogba and Mathias Fasssou Pogba. Both are twins, born on 19th August 1990 before their parents migrated to France.

Just like their younger brother, the twins are professional footballers, who also absorbed the love of the sport from their mother. While they have not had as huge an impact in football as the younger Pogba, they are still impressive on their own, having played for several clubs in Europe and America.

Mathias is currently a free agent, having been released by the Spanish side FC Lorca in recent months. He has appeared for several clubs such as Manchego, Tours, Sparta Rotterdam, and Patrick Thistle.

The third of Paul Pogba's brothers, Florentin, Plays for French Club Sochaux. Previously, he has appeared for Atlanta United, Sedan, Saint-Etienne, and others. The twins have also been called up to international duty time and again by the Guinea national team.

Marriage and children

Who is Paul Pogba wife? Does he have a child? The Manchester United star is not married. However, he is in a relationship with Bolivian Model Maria Zulay Salauses.

The couple met in 2017 and have been together since then. For a while, there have been rumours of their engagement and pending marriage. This was after she was seen wearing what appears to be an engagement ring at several public events with the United star. That being said, Maria and Paul Pogba have a child together, a son Labile Shakur.

With Paul Pogba’s son being only one-year-old, it is too early to predict what he will become. His name pays tribute to US rapper Tupac Shakur. However, if he is anything like his family, we can expect another footballer in a few years.

House

Since moving to Manchester United, Pogba has been permanently based in London. In 2017, he purchased a luxurious mansion in Cheshire, one of the city's affluent neighbourhoods.

The house costs a whopping £2.9million (though this is just a few week's pays for the United Star). It boasts of 5 bedrooms, a custom kitchen, a heated pool, sauna, media room, game room, and other luxurious amenities.

Pogba's cars
