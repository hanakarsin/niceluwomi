Ini Edo's Alleged Baby Daddy Sits With Our Pastor In Church

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, August 19, 2024

An Instagram user identified as DoreenPerky has reacted to the news of Award-winning Nollywood actress, Ini Edo allegedly welcoming a baby with a married man, Kome.

Perky who saw the picture of the man on an Instagram page disclosed that she had once reached out to the man identified as Kome to patronize her business.

She further stated that the man is wealthy, has luxuries of big cars and he has a special parking spot in the church.

The Instagram user disclosed that Kome attends Living Faith Church located in Badore, and sits with their pastor in the church.

According to Perky the alleged baby daddy of the actress’s child is also a worker in the church and this made her question the news that he is the father of the actress’s child.

See her post below:

“This life sha! these men would never support their church members or even patronize their business. I remember giving this man my flyer to patronise me,oga just rushed and collected the flyer and ignored me. I didn’t even know he’s this popular/Ini Edo’s baby daddy..and I really wanted him to support and patronise me based on south south brother but they prefer to spend money on these celebrities. Na wa!He’s very rich o..cause him get a G wagon, Range Rover and other big cars and he has a special parking spot in the church.

“Omo..I know this man dieeee!!! He attends my church o. Living Faith badore,he’s a very big man. He has different cars o,G wagon/all the big cars you can think of with customised plate number. He’s also a church worker. He’s from delta. are you sure this man is the father? cause he sits with pastors in our church o.”
