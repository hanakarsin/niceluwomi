DJ Kill Bill - Trivia, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, March 11, 2024

DJ who gained notoriety after documenting his suicide on Instagram. He ended his life on October 26th, 2014 after jumping into the Detroit River.

Before Fame

His given name was Billy Watts. He attended Renaissance High School in Detroit. 

Trivia

He released a mixtape before his death and certain tracks contained lyrics referencing his plans. 

Family Life

He'd lived in Detroit, Michigan.

Associated With

One of his posts is a selfie that features a Ghostface Killah poster in the background.
