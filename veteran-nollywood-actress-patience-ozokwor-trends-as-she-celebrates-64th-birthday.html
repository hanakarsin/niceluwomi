Veteran Nollywood Actress, Patience Ozokwor Trends As She Celebrates 64th Birthday

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Veteran Nollywood actress, Patience Ozokwor, popularly known as ‘Mama G’, is currently trending on the microblogging platform, Twitter, with over two thousand tweets as she marks her 64th birthday today.

Naija News reports that Patience is among the notable Nollywood stars that have graced the screen of movie lovers with stunning role interpretations, from ‘Ukwa’ to ‘Chief Daddy’ and many more.

Celebrating the new age, the actress who currently resides in Califonia, US with her daughter, took to her Instagram page to share adorable photos, rocking beautiful Ankara outfits.

Revealing what life has taught her, Patience said as people grow older, they begin to understand that life is not what an individual look like or owns.

According to mama G, life is about what a person has become and the people they have blessed.

She also expressed appreciation to God for the new age.

She wrote: “As you grow older, you start to understand more and more that life is not about what you look like or what you own, it’s all about the person you have become and the people you have blessed
All of my life, In every season, You are still God, I have a reason to sing, I have a reason to worship”
