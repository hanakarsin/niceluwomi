Ronnie Radke Isn't Married, but He's Dating Another Famous Person

Ronnie was previously with model Crissy Henderson. They broke up in 2013 because the singer reportedly cheated on her.

By Melissa Willets

Jun. 11 2024, Published 9:59 a.m. ET

As fans know, Ronnie Radke is a music artist known best as the lead singer of the band Falling in Reverse, but you may also remember that he was once part of the group Escape the Fate.

While his song "All My Life," featuring Jelly Roll, is making waves, fans are wondering about Ronnie's personal life — specifically if he's married and whether he has any kids. While we can share that the controversial star isn't betrothed to anyone presently, he has been in a long-term relationship with another famous person. He previously dated someone else and has a child as well.

Read on to find out who he's with now, and about his past romantic status — and what he says about fatherhood.

Ronnie Radke isn't married right now, but he has been dating a professional wrestler for a while.

Ronnie is in a committed relationship with WWE star Saraya Bevis, whom fans know as Paige.

Some fans will recall that Paige appeared on the reality show Total Divas starring the Bella twins, Nikki and Brie.

Ronnie and the wrestler have been together since 2018.

They are pretty private about their relationship, but Paige once talked about a terrifying incident involving a stalker who came to their home and how Ronnie detained the individual.

"Scariest thing ever," she said. "He told us symbols led him to us. But Ronnie got him in a hold until police got there. [What] is wrong with people… thank God Ronnie is around to protect our house. Jesus.”

Meanwhile, Paige isn't the first person who Ronnie has been in a serious relationship with. He was previously engaged to another woman with whom he had a child.

Ronnie Radke has a kid with Crissy Henderson.

Before coupling up with Paige, Ronnie was with model Crissy Henderson.

They broke up in 2013 because the singer reportedly cheated on her. But before that, they welcomed a child, a daughter, together.

Not much is known about the child, other than that her name is Willow Grace Radke. Ronnie has said that becoming a father definitely changed his life.

“It's like night and day," he told Loudwire in 2013. "My life has totally changed. It's like, if someone cuts me off in traffic, I used to think about stopping and pulling them out of their car to deal with them. Now that I've got a kid, I totally re-think moments like that. There are a lot of things I can't do anymore and that's probably a good thing.”

Of course, Ronnie is well known for having anger management issues.

But perhaps being a dad has chilled him out somewhat? At least, this is what he is claiming!

Meanwhile, it isn't known how much Willow is in her famous dad's life today, or whether he plans to marry Paige or have more children. As far as anyone knows, Willow's mom Crissy is currently single. Details of their co-parenting situation are also not known to the public.
