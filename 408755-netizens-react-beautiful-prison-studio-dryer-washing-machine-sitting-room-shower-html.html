Netizens React to Beautiful Prison with Studio, Dryer, Washing Machine, Sitting Room and Shower

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, July 7, 2024

- Social media users have expressed mixed reactions as photo and video emerged of a 5-star prison

- The exquisite prison boasts of a standard studio, nice eatery, sitting room with cushion chairs and so forth

- Some tweeps said they would gladly relocate to that prison; others thought it could equate to the abode of a rich man anywhere

Social media users have reacted to an emerging prison video with facilities usually found in a 5-star hotel.

The trending video, which was shared on Twitter by @ayemojubar, showed the inside of a mini prison facility in Norway.

In what came as a huge surprise to many internet users, the facility has a standard studio for recording music, an exquisite sitting room with cushion furniture, a TV set and a nice eatery.

Prisoners there also use washing machine, dryers, wardrobes and other benefits usually obtainable in well-to-do homes.

Netizens marvelled at the beautiful site; some frowned at it, stressing that having such in the country will only encourage crimes.

Her Majesty's Prison Addiewell in southern Scotland allows each of its resident inmates 40 weekly productive skill-building hours.
