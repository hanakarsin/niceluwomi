The country’s Vice President Samia Suluhu announced during a late-night press briefing that President John Pombe Magufuli lost the fight against debilitating heart disease.

Celebrities, citizens and fellow legislators from the country and across the continent expressed their shock, pain and heartache through touching posts on social media.

One man who was and still remains to be a diehard fan of the fallen president chose to get a permanent reminder of the man who led their country for six solid years.

The Magufuli fanatic had the leader’s face tattooed on his chest so he could look at it every day and never forget about the iron-fisted head of state.

It was meticulously done and captured the essence of Magufuli.

From his child-like smile, his strong jawline to his signature suit. Everything was drawn to perfection.

The man then recorded the tattooing process and shared it on social media.

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Tanzania's president John Pombe Magufuli had been missing from the public for about two weeks.

Speculation was rife about his whereabouts and health condition in the days leading to the announcement of his death.

His death was announced by vice president Samia Suluhu on Wednesday, March 17.

Magufuli reportedly succumbed to heart complications.
