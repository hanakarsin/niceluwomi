Saraki, Dankwambo, Pius Anyim, Others Meet PDP BoT Members

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Former Senate President, Bukola Saraki alongside other members of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) Reconciliation Committee on Thursday met with the party’s Board of Trustees (BoT).

Naija News reports that this was made known by Saraki in a post on his official Twitter handle.

The tweet reads: “As we continue to engage all critical stakeholders in our party, this morning, members of #PDPReconciliation & Strategy Committee and I are consulting with the highly respected members of the PDP Board of Trustees led by the Chairman of the BoT, Senator Walid Jibrin.”

Members of the reconciliation committee include one-time Senate President, Anyim Pius Anyim, and ex-governor of Cross River State Liyel Imoke as members.

Others are former Gombe Governor Ibrahim Dankwambo; ex-Katsina Governor Ibrahim Shema and Hon. Mulikat Akande

Recalls that the PDP committee on Wednesday met with former Governors elected on the platform of the party to resolve some crisis currently rocking the party.

The committee had on Tuesday met with former President Goodluck Jonathan.
