How to check cluster points in KUCCPS?

The KUCCPS student portal cluster points calculation is automatic. The portal automatically calculates your cluster points when you apply for a course. Once you log in to the portal, you will be able to see whether you qualify for the course or not.

What is the course code and program code?

A program and course code all mean the same. This is a code that identifies a specific academic program offered by a university or college. The codes differ from one course to another and different for various institutions in Kenya.

There are so many KUCCPS programme codes that come with varied categories of programmes offered by colleges across the country. For specific details about the programs and codes, it is advisable to check out the KUCCPS websites.

Tuko.co.ke explained how to use the NEMIS portal. The National Education Management Information System stores and manages data of learners and schools in Kenya. School managers and heads of institutions are responsible for ensuring learners and schools are registered on this portal.

The portal's primary role is to assist the Ministry of Education, development partners, National Treasury, KNEC, TSC, and other interested parties in collecting and analyzing data related to learners and schools for decision-making.
