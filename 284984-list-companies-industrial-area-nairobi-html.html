List of companies in Industrial Area Nairobi

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Nairobi industrial area companies started their operations way back in the colonial period. Believe it or not, the area holds the largest number of industries in the country and was mooted back in the 19th century but later re-planed in 1984. This is the plan that saw successive changes to the now referred industrial area, Nairobi. Industries dealing with bulk goods were located near the railway line. lighter industries were moved further inside based on the plan. If you are looking for some of the major companies in industrial area Nairobi, then you are in the right place.

The Nairobi industrial area is majorly surrounded by low-income residential areas. Some of these areas include Kaloleni, Makongeni, and Ofafa Jericho. The residents from these areas have been providing the required labor to the industries, which everyone knows that it is a basic factor for production. Due to the growing size of the area, it has made it difficult to upgrade the existing structures in the area. However, this has not hindered the hefty production that goes on in the area. Below is a breakdown of some of the industries found in the industrial area side by side with their crucial information you might want to know.
