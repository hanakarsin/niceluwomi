Mark Ferris - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, April 21, 2024

Content creator of challenges, tags and general vlogs on YouTube who has earned a subscriber base of more than 650,000. He has also posted advice videos, baking videos and fashion hauls on his channel. 

Before Fame

He registered for YouTube with the username TheBifflovesyou in February of 2010, but did not start posting his own content until years later.

Trivia

He was mentioned in a UK magazine called Shout as a "vlogger to watch" in 2016. He said in a November 2021 video that his goal as a content creator was to spread joy in a realistic way. 

Family Life

His sister was featured in a video he called "British Siblings Do the Stuff Your Mouth Challenge!!"

Associated With

He has collaborated with beauty guru Zoe Sugg in numerous YouTube videos.
