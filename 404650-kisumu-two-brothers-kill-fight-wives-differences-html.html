Kisumu: 2 brothers kill each other in fight over their wives' differences

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, July 19, 2024

- Joseph Othoro and his brother Kennedy Otieno engaged in a deadly fight on the night of Monday, March 1

- A police report confirmed the two brothers turned against each other following a fight between their wives

- The 37-year-old Othoro died on the same night while Otieno was pronounced dead the following morning

Kaniga Makome village in Muhoroni sub-county, Kisumu county, is reeling in shock following a bizarre incident that saw two brothers kill each other.

The residents mourned their sons Joseph Othoro and Kennedy Otieno but deep inside many questions remained unanswered on what exactly had befallen the quiet village.

The 37-year-old Othoro died on the night of Monday, March 1, while his younger brother Otieno, 36, was pronounced dead the following morning.

According to a police report seen by TUKO.co.ke, the two siblings had a deadly fight in the previous night, March 1, and sustained injuries that led to their deaths hours apart.

The seemingly deep seated differences played out when their wives first engaged in a similar fight on Sunday, February 28,which stoked anger between Othoro and Otieno later in the night.

The two brothers would later on turn against each other before both of them were rushed to the Jaramogi Oginga Odinga Teaching and Referral Hospital in Kisumu town.

Otieno passed away while being rushed to the hospital in the night while Othoro was confirmed dead later in the morning of Tuesday, March 2.

The body of the deceased, Benedina Nabwangu, was found on Wednesday, January 13, at 5am by her herdsboy, Benson Muhanji, in a pool of blood with a stab wound in the head.

The duo, a university student and her stepbrother who reportedly lived with their 65-year-old mother, fled without reporting the incident.

The university student was arrested while on her way to Nairobi and upon interrogation, claimed she escaped after their home was attacked by unknown people the fateful night.
